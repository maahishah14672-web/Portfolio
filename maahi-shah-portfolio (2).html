03
Strategic Simulation Google Ads Strategy

AI-Powered Lead Gen — Premium Dental Clinic

Challenge

A premium dental clinic wanted high-quality appointment bookings instead of low-value price inquiries from generic searches.

Strategy

Google Search + Performance Max with Smart Bidding (Maximize Conversion Value), offline conversion imports, premium-intent audience signals, and remarketing for booking-page visitors who didn't convert.

Execution

Keyword clusters around Invisalign, smile makeovers, premium dental — with deliberate exclusion of "cheap dentist" queries. Responsive search ads with conversion-focused messaging and optimized landing page CTAs.

Expected Outcomes

Higher quality leads, better appointment attendance, lower wasted ad spend, and smarter AI optimization through richer conversion data signals.
